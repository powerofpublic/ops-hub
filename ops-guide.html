Signing In

All tools use a single Google Sign-In. Sign in once and all tools are accessible in the same browser session.

  1. Go to powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub
  2. Click Sign in with Google
  3. Select your authorised Google account
  4. You are taken directly to the Operations Hub
Once signed in, your session stays active in that browser. You will not need to sign in again unless you sign out or clear your browser data.

To sign out, click the Sign out button in the top right corner of any tool. Use the ← Hub link to return to the portal without signing out.

The portal is your home base. It links to all tools and provides quick access to external resources.

Time Entry & Reporting Dashboard

Track billable hours, log expenses, generate invoices, and run reports across all your projects. All data syncs to Firebase in real time.

powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub/tracker.html

Dashboard

The Dashboard shows a real-time summary of your consulting activity. Stats include this week's hours, monthly billable revenue, active project count, non-billable hours and value, an hours-by-project bar chart, and your six most recent entries.

Timesheet

Shows your time entries week by week. Use Previous / Next to navigate between weeks.

  1. Click + Add Entry
  2. Select the date and enter hours
  3. Choose a project and task category
  4. Add a description of the work
  5. Click Save Entry

Click any row to edit or delete. Click Mark Invoiced to flag an entry as billed — click the green badge to undo.

Projects & Tasks

Every time entry, expense, and invoice is linked to a project. Click + New Project to create one.

Project StatusMeaning
Active — Bill to ClientBillable; counts toward revenue stats
Active — Non-billableActive but hours are not billed
Inactive — Non-billableCompleted or paused, no billing
Inactive — Billed to ClientCompleted and fully invoiced
Project TypeMeaning
Time and MaterialsHours billed at your hourly rate
Firm Fixed PriceFixed fee regardless of hours
RetainerMonthly flat fee — enter the retainer amount

Expenses

Click + Add Expense to log a business expense. Select a category, link to a project if billable, enter the amount (or use the mileage calculator), and set billable and reimbursed status. Export as CSV or QuickBooks IIF.

Reports

Generate summaries across any date range and project. Group by Project, Task, or Date. Export as CSV, Word (.doc), PDF, or QuickBooks IIF.

Invoice Generator

  1. Go to the Invoice tab — the number auto-generates (POP-YYYY-###)
  2. Set invoice date, due date, and your business information (saves for next time)
  3. Select a project to auto-fill client details
  4. Set the billing date range to pull in matching entries
  5. Add tax rate, payment terms, and notes as needed
  6. Click Generate Invoice
  7. Click Mark as Sent & Save to History to record it
  8. Click Print / PDF to save or send

The invoice header displays the Power of Public logo centered, with your tagline and website. Address and contact info appears in the Bill From section below.

Live Timer

The live timer in the top right corner tracks time in real time. Select a project, click play to start, pause to pause, and Log to convert elapsed time to a time entry. Requires at least one minute — rounds to the nearest quarter hour.

RFP Go / No-Go Decision Form

A structured framework for evaluating new business opportunities. Every submission is saved to Firebase as a permanent record.

powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub/go-no-go.html

Form Sections

SectionWhat to complete
01 Opportunity InformationAgency name, RFP number, funding, period of performance, contract type, scope, submission deadline
02 Pre-Submission PlanningConference date, Q&A link, Q&A deadline, questions to submit, participation instructions
03 Capability AnalysisPast experience, required skills, teaming needs, competitor landscape
04 Financial ProjectionsEstimated revenue, cost, margin, bid cost, probability of win
05 Evaluation CriteriaCriteria statements and weighting percentages
06 Final DecisionClick GO or NO-GO

Saving & Reviewing Records

Click ☁ Save Record at the bottom to save to Firebase. The Saved Records panel at the top of the page shows all past submissions. Click any record to load it back into the form for review or editing. Click ↺ Reset to clear the form without deleting saved records. Click ⎙ Print / PDF to save as a document.

Saving the same organization and RFP number combination updates the existing record rather than creating a duplicate.

Proposal Tracker

Manage active proposals from initial pursuit through award. Connects directly to the Go/No-Go form for seamless import.

powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub/proposal-tracker.html

Adding a Proposal

Click + Add Proposal. Fill in the organization name, RFP number, scope, submission deadline, funding value, status, probability of win, team role assignments (Proposal Lead, Technical Writer, Pricing/Budget, Subcontractor/Teaming Partner), and notes.

Importing from Go/No-Go

Click ⬇ Import from Go/No-Go to see all GO decisions not yet imported. Select one and click Import Selected →. The proposal form opens pre-filled with organization name, RFP number, scope, deadline, funding, and probability of win. Review, complete any missing fields, and save.

Statuses & Deadline Tracking

The table sorts by deadline, most urgent at the top. Deadline badges show days remaining: red under 7 days, amber under 21 days, green further out.

Project Manager

Manage tasks across all clients and projects with four views. Pulls project data directly from the Time Entry & Reporting Dashboard.

powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub/project-manager.html

Views

ViewBest for
🗂 KanbanSeeing all tasks by status at a glance. Drag-style column layout: To Do, In Progress, In Review, Complete, Blocked.
☰ ListSorting and scanning all tasks with full details. Sorted by due date, overdue highlighted in pink.
📊 TimelineVisualizing work across dates. Today marked by an amber line. Supports Excel export.
📅 CalendarSeeing tasks on a monthly calendar by due date.

Adding Tasks

  1. Click + Add Task
  2. Enter the task title
  3. Select a project — includes both Time Tracker projects (with codes and client names) and PM-only projects
  4. Set status, priority, due date, start date, assigned person, and category tag
  5. Click Save Task

Managing Projects

Click ⚙ Projects to open the project manager. Time Tracker projects appear automatically (read-only). Add custom PM projects with a name and color. Deleted PM projects do not delete associated tasks.

Timeline View & Excel Export

Switch to the Timeline tab. A date range bar appears below the toolbar — set From and/or To dates to focus the view. Use the Client, Project, Status, and Priority filters alongside the date range to narrow results. Click ⬇ Export Excel to download a .xlsx file of all currently filtered tasks, grouped by client with a header row per client. The filename includes active filters and date range automatically.

RFP Monitor

Search 49 procurement and grant sources, manage your full opportunity pipeline from discovery through award, and track your win rate over time.

powerofpublic.github.io/ops-hub/rfp-monitor.html

The RFP Monitor has its own dedicated guide with prompt rotation strategy, portal checklist, pipeline features, and troubleshooting.

→ Read the full RFP Monitor User Guide

Five Tabs

TabWhat it does
PipelineAll opportunities with live deadline badges, status tracking, notes, value estimates, and Import from Claude
SearchFive copy-ready prompts for Claude Pro — free, real-time web search, no API key
Manual Portals49 direct links organized into 9 sections with a weekly review checklist
Win / LossAutomatic scorecard showing win rate, total value, and outcome history
Email DigestWeekly pipeline digest export and saved prompt manager

How searching works

All searching is done through your existing Claude Pro subscription at claude.ai — completely free. Copy a prompt from the Search tab, paste it into Claude, and paste the results back into the Import from Claude box in the Pipeline tab. No API key, no per-search cost.

Key features

  • Deadline countdown badges — cards pulse red when under 7 days, amber under 21 days
  • Considering / Go No-Go status — shows a direct link to your Go/No-Go evaluation form
  • Duplicate detection — prevents the same opportunity being added twice
  • Portal weekly checklist — check off sites as you review them; resets every Monday
  • Weekly digest export — generates a full pipeline summary you can email or save
  • Saved searches — store up to 5 frequently used prompts or pipeline filters for quick reuse
  • Value tracking — estimated contract values sum to a total Pipeline $ in the header

Adding sources and portals

All 49 sources and portals are permanently stored in the HTML file — not your browser. To add or remove one, ask Claude with the name, URL, and type. Upload the updated file to GitHub and it's live everywhere instantly.

GitHub — Updating the Hub

All tool files live in your GitHub repository. Updating a file never touches your Firebase data.

github.com/powerofpublic/ops-hub
  1. Download the updated HTML file from Claude
  2. Rename it exactly — no spaces or numbers (e.g. tracker.html not tracker (1).html)
  3. Go to github.com/powerofpublic/ops-hub
  4. Click Add file → Upload files
  5. Drag the file in and click Commit changes
  6. Wait 60 seconds, then hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)

Troubleshooting

Common issues and fixes across all hub tools.

IssueFix
Spinning loading screenHard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R. Check internet connection. Open browser console (Cmd+Option+J) for error messages.
Sign-in button not workingCheck browser isn't blocking popups. Confirm powerofpublic.github.io is in Firebase authorized domains.
Data not showing after sign-inWait a few seconds for Firebase to load, then hard refresh. Check Firebase Console → Realtime Database → Data.
Changes not savingCheck internet connection. Open browser console for errors. Sign out and sign back in.
Site not updating after GitHub uploadWait up to 2 minutes for GitHub Pages to rebuild. Hard refresh or try incognito window.
File shows wrong version after uploadCheck the filename exactly matches — no spaces or numbers. GitHub requires exact filename match to replace.
Time Tracker projects missing in Project ManagerHard refresh the Project Manager. Confirm projects exist in the Time Tracker.
Invoice logo misalignedConfirm logo-pink.svg is uploaded to the ops-hub GitHub repo root alongside tracker.html.
