How to search for current RFPs
Use your Claude Pro subscription at claude.ai — free, included in your plan. Copy a prompt below, paste it into a new Claude chat, then paste the results into the Import from Claude box in the Pipeline tab.
Search prompts — copy and use in Claude Pro

Best day: Tuesday or Wednesday morning. Run 2–3 prompts per session for full coverage.

Federal & government
Search SAM.gov and Grants.gov for active RFPs in health communications, public health outreach, and community engagement posted in the last 14 days. For each result return: title, agency, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link.
Foundations & nonprofits
Search Philanthropy News Digest, CDC Foundation, Grantmakers in Health, NACCHO, and NNPHI for current RFPs and grant opportunities in health communications, social marketing, or behavior change consulting. For each result return: title, funder, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link.
Communications-specific
Search O'Dwyer's PR, Everything PR, and rfpdb.com for current RFPs in strategic communications, public health campaigns, or community outreach consulting. For each result return: title, organization, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link.
Regional — Maryland & DC
Search Maryland eMMA, DC OCP, Virginia EPROCURE, and HigherGov for active RFPs in health communications, public health education, or community engagement consulting posted in the last 30 days. For each result return: title, agency, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link.
Broad weekly sweep
Search the web for active RFPs and grant opportunities in health communications consulting, public health outreach, social marketing, behavior change campaigns, and community engagement posted in the last 14 days. Include federal, state, foundation, and nonprofit sources. For each result return: title, organization, type, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Return at least 8 results.
After getting results: Copy the full Claude response → Pipeline tab → paste into Import from Claude → click + Add all to pipeline.
