How to search for current RFPs
Use your Claude Pro subscription at claude.ai: free, included in your plan. Copy a prompt below, paste it into a new Claude chat, then paste the results into the Import from Claude box in the Pipeline tab.
Search prompts: copy and use in Claude Pro

Best day: Tuesday or Wednesday morning. Run 2-3 prompts per session for full coverage.

Federal & government
Search SAM.gov and Grants.gov for active RFPs in health communications, public health outreach, and community engagement posted in the last 30 days. For each result return: title, agency, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Foundations & nonprofits
Search Philanthropy News Digest, CDC Foundation, Grantmakers in Health, NACCHO, and NNPHI for current RFPs and grant opportunities in health communications, social marketing, or behavior change consulting. For each result return: title, funder, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Communications-specific
Search O'Dwyer's PR, Everything PR, and rfpdb.com for current RFPs in strategic communications, public health campaigns, or community outreach consulting. For each result return: title, organization, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Regional: Maryland & DC
Search Maryland eMMA, DC OCP, Virginia EPROCURE, and HigherGov for active RFPs in health communications, public health education, or community engagement consulting posted in the last 30 days. For each result return: title, agency, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Broad weekly sweep
Search the web for active RFPs and grant opportunities in health communications consulting, public health outreach, social marketing, behavior change campaigns, and community engagement posted in the last 30 days. Include federal, state, foundation, and nonprofit sources. For each result return: title, organization, type, deadline, 2-sentence description, and direct link. Return at least 8 results. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Public Health Communications Consultant
Search the web for active RFPs and solicitations using these exact phrases: "Seeks Public Health Communications Consultant Services," "Public Health Communications Consultant Services," and "Public Health Consultant Services." Include federal, state, foundation, and nonprofit sources. For each result return: title, organization, type (Government/Foundation/Nonprofit), deadline, 2-sentence description of the scope, and direct link to the listing.
Public awareness and behavior change
Search for active RFPs or grants in the last 30 days for public awareness campaigns, behavior change initiatives, or community outreach programs related to health, social services, or public sector engagement. Include title, issuer, deadline, summary, and link. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Community and equity-focused engagement
Find current RFPs or funding opportunities for community engagement, stakeholder engagement, or equity-focused outreach initiatives in health, education, or social impact sectors. Prioritize opportunities posted in the last 45 days. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Implementation support and technical assistance
Identify active solicitations for implementation support, technical assistance, or program delivery consulting that include communications, training, or stakeholder engagement components. Include direct links and deadlines. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Program enrollment and public participation
Search for RFPs or contracts focused on increasing program enrollment, service uptake, or public participation through marketing, outreach, or communications campaigns. Include government and nonprofit opportunities. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Subcontracting opportunities
Find recently awarded grants or contracts in public health, social services, or community programs where recipients may require subcontractors for communications, outreach, or stakeholder engagement. Include links to awardees. Include opportunities that may not be explicitly labeled as communications but involve outreach, engagement, training, or public-facing strategy.
Maternal and Black maternal health
Search for active RFPs, grants, or consulting opportunities in the last 45 days focused on maternal health, Black maternal health, birth equity, or perinatal health. Include opportunities from federal agencies, foundations, public health nonprofits, and community organizations. Look for contracts involving communications, community outreach, public awareness campaigns, technical assistance, or stakeholder engagement related to maternal and infant health outcomes. Include title, organization, deadline, description, and direct link.
